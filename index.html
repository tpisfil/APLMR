About Us

Welcome to AP Lawn Mower Repair!


We are a small family owned and operated small engine repair shop that is serving the greater Northern Virginia area. Founded in 2004, we are looking to serve our community by providing excellent customer service in timely fashion so that you do not have to spend anymore time without your lawn equipment. Our highly qualified technicians are trained to inspect, service, and repair both commercial & personal outdoor power equipment & small engines.
